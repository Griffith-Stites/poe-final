Sprint 1

Summary

In this sprint our goal was to design and sketch model a unit that can walk on a table so we can understand the dynamics of a leg walking robot.

Progress

Sketch model robot
Robot at the end of Sprint 1

By the end of Sprint 1, we had a walking sketch model complete with integrated mechanical, electrical, and software. We had a design for the next prototype for use in Sprint 2. We also wrote a preliminary library to control legs, which was later scrapped in the name of simplicity.

Learning

  • Legs need some sort of friction on the bottom, otherwise they will slide.
  • Need an Arduino Mega to have enough PWM pins to support all eight servos.
  • If we are not careful, the robot could be too heavy and it may not stick with magnets.
